About Our Sermon Library

Welcome to the Fellowship Baptist Church sermon library, a searchable collection of messages that faithfully proclaim the truths of God's Word. This resource is designed to help you study, grow, and deepen your understanding of the Bible.

With our library, you can:

  • Search through sermon transcripts to find specific topics, Bible verses, or teachings
  • Ask questions about sermon content and receive AI-generated answers based on the preaching
  • Watch video sermons with synchronized transcripts for better comprehension
  • Browse sermons by date, topic, or Scripture reference

The Gospel Message

At Fellowship Baptist Church, we believe that the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and inerrant Word of God. We preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ, which is the power of God unto salvation to everyone that believeth (Romans 1:16).

The Bible teaches that all have sinned and come short of the glory of God (Romans 3:23), and the wages of sin is death (Romans 6:23). But God commendeth his love toward us, in that, while we were yet sinners, Christ died for us (Romans 5:8).

Salvation is by grace through faith in the Lord Jesus Christ (Ephesians 2:8-9). If you confess with your mouth the Lord Jesus, and believe in your heart that God has raised Him from the dead, you will be saved (Romans 10:9).
