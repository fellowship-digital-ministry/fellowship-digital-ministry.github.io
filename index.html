About This Ministry

Fellowship Digital Ministry provides tools to help you explore and study sermon content from Fellowship Baptist Church. This project aims to make sermon content more accessible and searchable through technology.

This ministry was created to support the church's mission of "Exalting our Saviour ~ Edifying the Believer ~ Evangelizing the World".
