About the Sermon Library

"And how shall they preach, except they be sent? as it is written, How beautiful are the feet of them that preach the gospel of peace, and bring glad tidings of good things!"

Romans 10:15 (KJV)

Our Mission

The Fellowship Baptist Church Sermon Library exists to provide a searchable collection of Biblical preaching that glorifies God, edifies believers, and reaches the lost with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

We believe that God's Word is living and powerful (Hebrews 4:12), and that faith comes by hearing, and hearing by the word of God (Romans 10:17). Our mission is to make sound Biblical preaching accessible to as many people as possible through modern technology.

What Makes This Library Unique

Our sermon library is designed with several distinctive features:

  • Full-Text Search: Every sermon has been transcribed and indexed, allowing you to search for specific words, phrases, or Bible references across the entire collection.
  • AI-Powered Question Answering: Ask questions about sermon content and receive relevant answers drawn directly from the preaching.
  • Synchronized Transcripts: Read along with the sermon audio/video with our timestamped transcripts.
  • Topic and Scripture Organization: Find sermons by topic or Bible passage to study specific subjects.

About Fellowship Baptist Church

Fellowship Baptist Church is an independent, fundamental Baptist church dedicated to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ and teaching the Bible as the inspired, inerrant Word of God.

We stand firm on the fundamental doctrines of the faith and maintain a traditional approach to worship and ministry. Our church is committed to:

  • The verbal, plenary inspiration of the Scriptures
  • The deity, virgin birth, and bodily resurrection of Jesus Christ
  • Salvation by grace through faith alone
  • The local church as God's plan for this age
  • The Great Commission to spread the Gospel worldwide
  • The King James Version of the Bible for all teaching and preaching

For more information about Fellowship Baptist Church, please visit our main website.

Technical Information

This sermon library was created using modern technology to make God's Word more accessible:

  • Sermon audio is extracted from our YouTube channel
  • Transcription is performed using advanced AI speech recognition
  • Content is indexed and made searchable using vector embeddings
  • User questions are answered using natural language processing

While we use technology to enhance accessibility, we remain committed to the timeless truth of God's Word and the traditional methods of expository preaching.
