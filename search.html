About This Tool

What Is This?

This is a simple way to find answers from Pastor's sermons. It searches through all the sermon videos our church has recorded and finds exactly where the Pastor has spoken about your question.

Think of it as having all of Pastor's sermons in one place where you can ask questions and get answers in his own words.

"Study to shew thyself approved unto God, a workman that needeth not to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth." — 2 Timothy 2:15 (KJV)

How It Works

When you type a question:

  1. The tool searches through all of Pastor's sermon videos
  2. It finds the parts where he talks about your question
  3. It gives you an answer based only on what Pastor has actually said
  4. It shows you links to watch those exact moments in his sermons

Important: All answers come directly from Pastor's own sermons. This isn't someone else's interpretation - it's what Pastor himself has taught.

"Prove all things; hold fast that which is good." — 1 Thessalonians 5:21 (KJV)

How To Use This Tool

Here are some helpful tips:

  • Ask specific questions, like "What does Pastor teach about prayer?"
  • Include Bible verses if you want to know about a specific passage
  • Click on "Watch Video" to see Pastor teaching about your question
  • If you don't get a helpful answer, try asking in a different way
  • You can ask follow-up questions to learn more

"The heart of the prudent getteth knowledge; and the ear of the wise seeketh knowledge." — Proverbs 18:15 (KJV)

Questions You Can Ask

Here are some examples to get you started:

  • "How does a person get to heaven?"
  • "What is the Trinity?"
  • "How should Christians live?"
  • "Why read the King James Bible?"
  • "Who is Melchizedek?"

Need Help?

If you have any questions about using this tool or run into problems, please speak with someone from our church tech team who will be happy to assist you.
