About This Sermon Search Tool

What Is This Tool?

This tool enables you to explore and learn from the Pastor's biblical teachings through a simple conversation interface. By asking questions about scripture, doctrine, or Christian living, you'll receive responses drawn directly from sermon content with references to the exact sermon moments where topics are discussed.

All answers come directly from Pastor's own words with Scripture references from the King James Bible.

"Study to shew thyself approved unto God, a workman that needeth not to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth." — 2 Timothy 2:15 (KJV)

How To Use This Tool

Follow these simple steps:

  • Type your Bible question in the chat box below
  • Receive answers with direct quotes from sermons
  • Click "Show Sources" to see which sermons address your question
  • Watch video clips or full sermons with the "Watch Video" button
  • Ask follow-up questions to explore topics in greater depth

"Prove all things; hold fast that which is good." — 1 Thessalonians 5:21 (KJV)

Tips For Better Results

For the most helpful responses:

  • Be specific in your questions
  • Include Scripture references when possible
  • The Pastor uses the King James Version for Bible verses
  • Click on Bible references to read the full passages
  • You can select your preferred language from the dropdown menu

Sample Questions

Here are some examples of questions you can ask:

  • How does a person get to heaven?
  • What is the Trinity?
  • How should Christians live in today's world?
  • Why read the King James Bible?
  • What does Romans 8 teach about predestination?
